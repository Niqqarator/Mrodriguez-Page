Miguelangel Rodriguez better known as "Mrodriguez" is a young producer & dj borned in La Guaira, Venezuela. Known by his great perfomance and extensive repertoire styles at the time to producing and mixing, his carreer like a producer began in 2018 and release his first track under the seal of the popular spanish record label “Distance Music” having a great receivement. Influenced by different sounds, Mrodriguez has built along his carreer an unique escense, being able to move between musical genres that go from minimal tech groove to melodic and darkest Afro House. Being able to sign with big records label of minimal house and at the same time take the highest places of the top afro and organic house.
